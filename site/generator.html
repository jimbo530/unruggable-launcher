Risks & Disclosures
Risks you should understand:
• Smart contract risk: This contract is unaudited. Bugs could cause loss of deposited LP.
• Impermanent loss: LP positions can lose value when token prices diverge. You may withdraw less than you deposited.
• Variable yield: Aave deposit rates change constantly. Yield is not guaranteed.
• Irreversible burns: Paired tokens are permanently destroyed. This cannot be undone.
• Lock periods: If you choose a lock, you cannot withdraw until it expires. No exceptions.
• Admin pause: The contract admin can pause operations. Withdrawals remain available when paused.
• Not a stablecoin: Money for Trees is a USDC deposit receipt, not a stablecoin or security.
