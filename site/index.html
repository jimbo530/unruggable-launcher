How It Works

Three steps from dollars to a fully autonomous token economy with perpetual volume.

1

Seed

Deposit $5+. Your seed funds atomic token creation, initial liquidity, and reactor pool seeding. No presale. No team allocation.

2

Launch

One transaction creates your token, locks liquidity permanently, and connects to the reactor network. Ownership is renounced at birth.

3

Grow

Reactors cycle volume perpetually across connected pools. Every swap in the network generates activity for your token. Set it and forget it.
