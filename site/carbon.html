How It Works

CHAR tokens are Puro.earth certified biochar carbon removal credits, tokenized on-chain by Toucan. Each CHAR burned permanently retires 1 tonne of CO2 from the atmosphere.

CCC (Carbon Counting Credits) on Polygon represent 1 lb of CO2 equivalent biochar sequestration each. Minting tracks verified real-world impact.

MfT (Memes for Trees) is the network heartbeat token. 1% of every NFT purchase is used to buy and burn MfT, creating permanent deflationary pressure that funds the ecosystem.

Reactor Burns permanently retire tokens by compounding LP fees. The CHAR reactor burns CHAR forever, removing carbon credits from circulation permanently.
