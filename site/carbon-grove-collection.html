Create this collection's vault

A collection vault is a fresh no-exit locker contract — the exact same verified contract every Carbon Grove NFT uses. It has no function that can move tokens out: any carbon sent in is locked forever.

• One-time — one vault per collection, for the whole community.
• Deployed from your wallet — you pay the network fee (about a cent) and the vault is registered to this collection permanently.
• Non-custodial — nobody controls the carbon inside. Not you, not us, not the collection owner. It can never come back out.

Retire today: your USDC buys biochar carbon — CHAR (1 token = 1 tonne CO₂e) or CCC (1 token = 1 lb CO₂e) — on Base at the live pool price, and it lands directly in the collection's vault. That contract has no way to move tokens out, so the carbon is retired forever, credited to your chosen NFT on the board.

The CHAR pool is deep (low price impact). The CCC pool is still small — buying CCC costs more than spot but helps seed its market. The slippage guard shows you the price impact before you commit; you decide.
Endow forever: your USDC is minted 1:1 into CHAR-R / CCC-R retirement-fund tokens, locked into the collection's vault permanently. The fund's lending yield keeps buying and retiring carbon on its own, forever, and part compounds so the endowment grows. Nobody — not you, not any NFT owner — is ever paid; the yield only retires carbon.

Note: CHAR-R retires fully. A CCC-R endowment locks and grows fine, but its retirement trickle is limited until the CCC pool deepens — the principal is safe and counted either way.
