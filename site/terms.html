2. Description of Service

The Service provides a smart-contract interface for deploying ERC-20 tokens on the Base blockchain with permanently locked liquidity. Specifically:

  • A factory contract creates a new token and 2 locked liquidity pools (TOKEN/Money 70% + TOKEN/Meme 30%) plus 1 SporeReactor. 50% of reactor fees burn the token, 50% go to the launcher's wallet.
  • All liquidity positions are locked inside SporeReactor contracts that contain no withdrawal function. Liquidity is locked by the absence of code, not by a timelock or promise.
  • All contracts are verified on Basescan and contain no admin, owner, mint, or pause functions.

The Service interacts with immutable smart contracts. Once a token is launched, neither MfT nor anyone else can modify, pause, or reverse the transaction.
