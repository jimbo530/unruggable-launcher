HOW ADOPTION WORKS
Three steps. Your token keeps its community. It just becomes permanent.
Step 1

Adopt ($5+)

Paste your token address, choose your deposit amount ($5 minimum), and supply tokens to pair. Two reactors are cloned — one for your token, one for CHAR. No migration needed.

Step 2

Add Pools

Use the Card Shop to create LP pools ($5 each). Each pool pairs your token with another asset and locks the LP in your reactor forever.

Step 3

Reactor Fires

Every 2 hours, anyone calls execute(). Fees are collected, tokens burned, liquidity deepened. Supply shrinks. Floor rises. Forever.

Step 4

Invite Others

Share your invite link. Anyone who adopts through it chains their reactor upstream to yours. 5% of their fees fuel your burns.
