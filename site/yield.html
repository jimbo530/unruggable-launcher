How yield works:
1. USDC deposited into Money for Trees earns Aave yield
2. Anyone calls Harvest to split yield: 33% holders, 33% charity, 33% service
3. Holder share is distributed proportionally to all MfT holders
4. LP pools hold MfT — register your pool so LP holders can claim their share
5. Claim sends your earned MfT directly to your wallet

Yield rates vary — based on Aave USDC rates. Not guaranteed.
