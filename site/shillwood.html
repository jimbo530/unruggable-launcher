FAQ
What does "unrugable" actually mean?

The liquidity backing your token is held inside a reactor smart contract that has no function to withdraw LP positions. The code literally cannot remove them. It's not a timelock, not a multisig, not a promise. The withdraw function doesn't exist.

Why "Shillwood"?

Sherwood Forest, on the Robin Hood chain. You shill, the forest holds the loot, and nobody — sheriff or launcher — can steal the pot. The liquidity stays in the greenwood forever.

How much does it cost to launch?

Free. You only pay gas on Robinhood Chain. The factory creates 2 locked pools and a reactor in one transaction. No seed money, no pre-sale, no fee.

How do I earn from my token?

Every 2 hours, anyone can fire the reactor. It collects trading fees, then splits your token's share: 50% is burned (supply goes down), 50% is sent to your wallet. More trading volume = more earnings for you. It's automatic and permissionless.

What are the two pools?

TOKEN/FTP (70% of supply) — paired against Feed The People, a deposit receipt backed 1:1 by USDG whose vault yield feeds people. TOKEN/MfT (30% of supply) — paired against Meme for Trees, the ecosystem meme token with its own reactor network. Two independent markets, loosely coupled by arb bots.

What is FTP (Feed The People)?

FTP is a deposit receipt backed 1:1 by USDG held in a vault. The vault's yield is split three ways on every harvest — operations, the Meme Reactor, and registered feeding partners. It trades near $1 and every swap through your FTP pool generates the volume that feeds the flow.

What is MfT (Meme for Trees)?

Meme for Trees is the ecosystem meme token, bridged to Robinhood Chain. It has its own reactor network that burns MfT and pushes floors up over time. The MfT pool gives your token exposure to that momentum.

Can anyone call the reactor?

Yes. execute() is permissionless. Anyone can trigger a burn+pay+compound cycle after the 2-hour cooldown.

Is this the same code as the Base launcher?

Same doctrine, ported to Uniswap V4 (Robinhood Chain's venue). Launch flow, supply, market cap, 70/30 split, reactor burn/pay/compound cycle, and the no-withdraw lock are identical to the Unrugable Launcher live on Base. The only changes are the V4 plumbing the venue forces.
