How It Works

1Paste any Base token's contract address and search
2Seed a vault (first time) or deposit USDC into an existing one
3Your USDC mints Money for Trees — a 1:1 deposit receipt backed by USDC in Aave
4Half the Money swaps for your token. Both sides go into a permanent liquidity pool
5Aave yield on the Money side splits three ways: 1/3 funds tree planting, 1/3 to holders (adds to your position), 1/3 to the network
6Your community climbs the leaderboard. Withdraw anytime — I Love Money (USDC) or I Love Meme (your token)
Why it matters: Every $10 deposited funds roughly 1 tree per year — forever, as long as the deposit stays. Your meme community builds real liquidity while planting real trees. No donations. No rugs. Just yield doing good.
