WHAT IS MYCOPAD
A token launchpad where every token is backed by permanently locked liquidity, a deflationary reactor, and a network that gets stronger with every launch.

Unruggable By Design

Your seed ETH backs 3 floor pools (AZUSD, cbBTC, WETH) with real two-sided liquidity. 95% of supply sits as a single-sided MfT curve above the floor. All 4 LP positions lock in a Spore Reactor with no withdraw function. Locked by code, not by promise.

Deflationary Reactor

Every trade generates fees. The Spore Reactor collects those fees, burns your token (reducing supply permanently), then buys back more and deposits it as deeper liquidity. The floor price ratchets up with every trade cycle.

The Mycelium Network

10% of every reactor's fees flow upstream to MfT Reactor Prime. Every spore feeds the network. Larger launches join the mycelium mesh and receive connections from future launches, creating perpetual cross-pollination.

One Transaction

You pick a name, symbol, and supply. MycoPad handles everything in a single tx: deploy the token, create 3 floor pools + 1 MfT curve, clone a reactor, lock all 4 positions, and connect to the mycelium. Done in ~30 seconds.

FAQ
What does "unruggable" actually mean?

The liquidity backing your token is held inside a Spore Reactor smart contract that has no function to withdraw LP positions. The code literally cannot remove them. It's not a timelock, not a multisig, not a promise. The withdraw function doesn't exist.

How much does it cost to launch?

The minimum seed is about $1 worth of ETH (~0.0003 ETH). That creates 3 floor pools + 1 MfT curve with real liquidity. Larger seeds create deeper floors. The launch itself is free — you pay only the seed ETH and gas.

What do I get as the launcher?

You get 0 tokens. MycoPad is a free, zero-allocation launchpad — 5% into floor pools, 95% into the MfT curve. 0% to anyone. The factory stays admin of the Spore Reactor. No team tokens, no hidden wallets.

How does the reactor make the floor go up?

Every trade on your token's pools generates fees. The reactor collects those fees, burns your token (permanently reducing supply), buys back more with the other side, and deposits both back as deeper liquidity. Less supply + more liquidity = higher floor. Anyone can call execute() to trigger a cycle every 2 hours.

What is the mycelium mesh?

Tokens launched with enough seed (0.4+ ETH) join the mycelium mesh. Future launches can create cross-token pools (hyphae) connecting to mesh-eligible spores. This means new launches bring trading volume to older tokens, and the network effect compounds. 10% of every reactor's fees also flow upstream to MfT Reactor Prime, feeding the whole ecosystem.

What is MfT?

MfT (MemeForTrees) is the ecosystem heartbeat. Every launch creates a TOKEN/MfT curve with 95% of supply locked in a reactor. When MfT Reactor Prime fires, the price wave cascades through every TOKEN/MfT pool — generating fees, burning supply, feeding the whole network. MfT is the root of the mycelium.

Can anyone call the reactor?

Yes. execute() is permissionless. Anyone can trigger a burn+compound cycle after the 2-hour cooldown. The reactor pays for itself through the value it creates — there's no keeper needed, though anyone can be rewarded by front-running the compound with a trade.

What chains is MycoPad on?

Currently live on Base. More chains coming as the network grows.

Is the code verified?

Yes. All contracts are verified on BaseScan. The factory, reactor implementation, and every cloned reactor are readable on-chain. Don't trust us — read the code.
