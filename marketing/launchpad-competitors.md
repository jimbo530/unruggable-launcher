# Base Launchpad Competitors (May 2026)

Source: Grok research

## Active Competitors

### Clanker (clanker.world / @clanker_world)
- **Launch fee:** Free (gas only)
- **Post-launch:** ~1% tx fee (40% to creator, rest to protocol/treasury)
- **Volume:** Hundreds to thousands weekly, ~$985K in 30-day fees
- **Mechanic:** AI bot deploys on command via Farcaster/Telegram, direct to Uniswap V3
- **Liquidity:** Permanent locks, no bonding curve migration
- **Strength:** Volume, social integration, free to launch, AI-native
- **Weakness:** No burn mechanic, no impact angle, pure meme factory

### Flaunch.gg
- **Launch fee:** Near-zero (gas + ~$30 in some configs)
- **Post-launch:** 1% swap fee (100% to creators/devs + buybacks)
- **Volume:** Dozens to hundreds, $2M+ 24h volume
- **Mechanic:** Uniswap V4 hooks, fair fixed-price entry, no presales
- **Strength:** Fair-launch focus, creator revenue, V4 innovation
- **Weakness:** No burn mechanic, no reactor network, no impact

### Pump.fun
- **Chain:** Solana only (no confirmed Base deployment)
- **Status:** Not a Base competitor yet

## Our Strengths (things they CAN'T copy easily)
1. **reactor network** — network effect they'd need years to build
2. **Carbon credit removal** — CHAR removed from markets permanently, real environmental impact
3. **2 locked pools + 1 reactor per launch** — deeper liquidity from day 1
4. **5 immutable reactors** — credibility via code, not promises
5. **Ecosystem integration** — every launch feeds the MfT burn engine

## Our Weaknesses (honest assessment)
1. **Low awareness** — free launch is incredibly competitive but nobody knows
2. **No social bot** — Clanker's Farcaster bot is a killer feature
3. **Low volume** — 12 launches vs thousands
4. **Discovery** — no trending page, no real-time launch feed
