# What Unrugable Offers Partners

## Free Permanent Liquidity for Your Token

Meme for Trees started as sharing memes on X to fund tree planting. No purchase necessary. Then we built tools — a free token launcher, reactor network, charity vaults, games — and launched MfT as a meme on Bankr. The tools are open to anyone.

The Unrugable reactor network is a set of on-chain contracts on Base that fire every 2 hours, cycling fees across hundreds of Uniswap V3 pools. Five reactors are fully renounced (immutable). Add-only admin keys. No multisig. No off switch.

---

## What Partners Get

- **Free launch with earnings.** Launch costs nothing — just gas. Your reactor pays you 50% of token fees every 2 hours. The other 50% gets burned, reducing supply.
- **Permanent locked liquidity.** LP positions are locked inside reactor contracts. There is no withdraw function. Liquidity cannot be removed — ever.
- **Two-pool design.** 70% of supply pairs against Money for Trees (semi-stable, Aave yield funds trees). 30% pairs against Meme for Trees (wild meme with reactor heartbeat). Stable floor + meme upside.
- **Trading routes across the network.** Your token connects to a network of hundreds of pools. More pairs means more routes for traders and bots, which generates volume.
- **Fee cycling every 2 hours.** Reactors collect V3 trading fees, burn supply, pay launchers, and cascade fees through the network.
- **On-chain transparency.** Every reactor firing, every swap is verifiable. No dashboards to trust. Read the chain.

## How Pairing with MfT Helps Your Token

All launched tokens pair against Money for Trees and Meme for Trees in the reactor stack. This is mutual:

- **Your token** gets permanent liquidity, automated fee cycling, and trading routes through hundreds of connected pools.
- **Meme for Trees** gets more pairs, more fee surfaces, more network activity.
- **Trees** get funded automatically. Money for Trees generates Aave yield from its USDC backing.

This is not a promise about price. It's plumbing that creates trading activity and funds impact as a byproduct of normal DeFi behavior.
