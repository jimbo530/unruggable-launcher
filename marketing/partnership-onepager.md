# What Unrugable Offers Partners

## Permanent Liquidity Tools for Your Token — Free

Meme for Trees started as sharing memes on X to fund tree planting. No purchase necessary. Then we built tools — a token launcher, reactor network, charity vaults, games — and launched MfT as a meme on Bankr. The tools are open to anyone.

The Unrugable reactor network is a set of on-chain contracts on Base that fire every 2 hours, cycling fees across hundreds of Uniswap V3 pools. Five reactors are fully renounced (immutable). Add-only admin keys. No multisig. No off switch.

---

## What Partners Get

- **Permanent locked liquidity.** LP positions are locked inside reactor contracts. There is no withdraw function. Liquidity cannot be removed — ever.
- **Trading routes across the network.** Your token gets paired into a network of hundreds of pools. More pairs means more routes for traders and bots to arbitrage across, which generates volume for your token and the network.
- **Fee cycling every 2 hours.** Reactors collect V3 trading fees, swap cross-tokens, and redistribute. This creates automated trading activity across connected pools — not a price guarantee, but a mechanism that keeps markets active.
- **Arb surfaces.** Each new pool creates price discrepancies that attract bot volume. One organic trade can ripple across connected pairs. More pools, more routes, more activity.
- **On-chain transparency.** Every reactor firing, every swap is verifiable. No dashboards to trust. Read the chain.
- **Mandatory charity fund wall.** Every launch gets a charity fund sell wall — helping others is coded into the architecture. Those sell wall LPs earn charity fund token yield, locking more into the pools over time. Charity deposits move to non-refundable positions automatically.

## How Pairing with MfT Helps Your Token

All launched tokens pair against MfT in the reactor stack. This is mutual:

- **Your token** gets trading routes through hundreds of connected pools. Market volatility between MfT and your token creates arb opportunities that generate volume you wouldn't have alone.
- **MfT** gets more pairs, more fee surfaces, more activity. The meme token benefits from a bigger network.
- **Charity** gets funded automatically. The architecture moves charity fund deposits into permanently locked LP positions. No one can pull them. Impact grows with every launch.

This is not a promise about price. It's plumbing that creates trading activity and funds charity as a byproduct of normal DeFi behavior.

## What It Costs

Nothing to become a reactor pair. No fee, no rev-share, no token allocation.

The reactor network benefits from more pairs because more pairs means more fee surfaces and more trading routes. You benefit from permanent liquidity infrastructure. Charity benefits from every transaction.

## How to Integrate (4 Steps)

1. **Create a V3 LP position** pairing your token with MfT (or another network token like POOP, CHAR, BB, EB).
2. **Send the LP position** to the appropriate reactor contract. The reactor locks it permanently.
3. **Reactor owner calls `addPool()`** to register the new pool. Fires on the next 2-hour cycle.
4. **Done.** No maintenance, no monitoring. The keeper bot handles execution. Anyone can call `execute()` permissionlessly.
