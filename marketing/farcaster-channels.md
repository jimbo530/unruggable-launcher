## Engagement Rules

1. Post between 14:00-16:00 UTC (peak Farcaster DeFi activity)
2. Reply to every comment within 4 hours
3. Like and reply to 3 relevant posts in each channel before posting your own
4. Never double-post the same content to multiple channels — adapt the framing
5. Use $MfT cashtag naturally, never force it
6. Link to verifiable on-chain data whenever claiming stats
7. If a post gets >5 likes, follow up with a deeper thread in the same channel
