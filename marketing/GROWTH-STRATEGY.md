## Executive Summary

MfT has a unique structural advantage: the Reactor Heartbeat is a provable, on-chain buy+burn engine that fires every 2 hours. No other DeFi protocol offers automated, predictable, verifiable buy pressure. This is the core marketing message.

Growth channels prioritized by cost (free first):

1. **Agent Economy** — 40% of on-chain tx are now AI agents. They're our primary audience.
2. **Farcaster/Base Social** — Free organic reach in niche channels where builders gather

4. **Cross-Protocol Partnerships** — Complementary projects on Base
5. **Game Funnels** — Baselings + Arcade as consumer onramps
6. **Token Adoption** — Orphan tokens revived via Shark agent, proving the network grows permissionlessly
