Your hold is a budget — split it between cargo and rooms. Every room you bolt in eats hold tonnage but adds function: guns, bunks, a galley, a sick bay. A warship packed with cannon hauls almost nothing; a bare keelboat is all cargo, no teeth. Pick your hull, then fit it out.
