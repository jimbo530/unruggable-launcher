Pick a rival crew to fight on the open sea. You fight a snapshot of their pawn — their stats and equipped kit, AI-piloted. No live opponent, no waiting. Your recruited fighter (the active one from your Crew) takes the field against them.

🌊 This is the open sea — real stakes. Unlike harbor sparring, gear that drops in an open-sea fight is lost to the water (no take-backs). Choose your rival, then sail out.
