Goblin Cave

✦ a day's voyage out — a tougher fight, bigger loot ✦

Beyond the harbour, a goblin warren is bored into the headland a short march EAST of Port Royal. The pack is small but well-dug-in — a slinger on the high rock, blades at the chokepoint. Clear the cave and the loot pool pays your cut.

⛵ A day out from the port. Tougher than the bilge — bring a hero who's found their feet in the arena first.
