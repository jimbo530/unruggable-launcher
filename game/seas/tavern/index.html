💧 The Galley — water your hero to grow its stats

Sit a hero down and give it water. The water becomes part of your hero and stays — it's their strength, locked in, and it grows their stats for the Decks. The more you pour, the stronger they get. This is plain Water, so it strengthens them all-round — a little to every stat.

Want to pump one stat hard? Send your hero to Town Work — each job pours its own water: 💪 haul cargo → Strength · 🛡️ stock rations → Stamina · 🏹 mend nets → Dexterity · 🔭 tend the lighthouse → Intellect · 🐚 sea-calling → Wisdom · 💰 barter at market → Charm
